Self-Motivation

Motivation is an important skill that can bring positive results. Motivating others is one of my key strengths, this is a important skill. It will lead to growth for both me and the company.

Problem Solving

Problem solving is my critical thinking skill that involves analyzing a problem, generating and implementing a solution, and assessing the success of the plan. I can to come up with practical solutions.

Team Work

An important teamwork skill is being able to mediate problems between team members. I can to negotiate with my team members to settle disputes and make sure everyone is happy with the team’s choices.

Adaptability

I am a people who adapt well rarely feel the pressure to quit. Every challenge is exciting, and remaining dedicated to their job means pushing through even when things get hard. Likewise, I am able to stay positive and encourage their team members to stay focused during difficult times.

Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence (EI) is the ability of an individual to understand my own emotions and the emotions of others.

Time Management

Time Management is important, because all tasks have to be done at specific times. Time Management affects my job day, week, month, as well as other people’s work flow.
